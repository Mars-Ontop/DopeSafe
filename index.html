Welcome To DopeSafe!

> DopeSafe's current goal in mind is to improve the global
understanding of the importance to practice cyber security. We
believe that with the current flaws in privacy nations and three
letter agencies provide, there is no privacy in which we should be
entitled as a basic right whilst browsing the web.

> Some tools on this site may have malicious purpose; however
we do not encourage any followers to be malicious, we are simply
an educational resource for those who wish to start practing security.
Any "hacktivism" or ill-intended acts are not related to us and never
will be. We stand alone in this goal.

> Any media that isn't displayed on this site isnt us!
IF any channels / servers are distributed under our name, just know we
don't have any control over what they do or say. For this reason, we highly
recommend joining random media that has our branding. We also highly advise
not running tools from them as it's likely malware.
